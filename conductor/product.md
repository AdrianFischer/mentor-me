# Product Guide: Assisted Intelligence

## Initial Concept
Assisted Intelligence is a keyboard-driven task management application that blends high-speed productivity with deep AI integration. It is designed to be more than just a list, acting as a strategic partner and mentor for its users.

## Target Users
- **Power Users:** Individuals who value efficiency and prefer keyboard-driven interfaces for rapid interaction.
- **Developers:** Professionals looking for an AI-integrated workflow that can potentially be extended.
- **General Productivity Seekers:** Users who want a simple yet powerful tool to manage their daily tasks without friction.

## Core Goals
- **Keyboard-First Efficiency:** Provide a lightning-fast experience where every core action is accessible via shortcuts.
- **Deep AI Integration:** Seamlessly weave Gemini into the daily workflow, making AI an active participant in task management.
- **Strategic Mentorship:** Create a personalized "Mentor" with deep memory of the user's career status, challenges, and long-term goals to facilitate strategic, high-level discussions.

## Key Features
- **Rapid Keyboard Navigation:** Dedicated shortcuts for adding, editing, moving, and completing tasks.
- **Mentor Mode:** A specialized chat interface that leverages persistent user context to provide career guidance and strategic next steps.
- **Autonomous Agents:** AI capabilities that proactively break down complex projects and verify task completion.
- **Local-First Architecture:** Ensures speed and privacy with robust local persistence (Isar) and optional cloud synchronization.

## Mentor Personality
The Mentor is primarily **Analytical and Objective**, providing data-driven insights and structured frameworks. It is also **Direct and Challenging**, pushing the user to think strategically and questioning choices that may not align with their long-term goals.

## Visual Identity
Assisted Intelligence follows a **Minimalist and Distraction-Free** aesthetic. It prioritizes high-contrast UI, clean typography, and the removal of visual clutter to keep the focus entirely on the user's work and strategy.
